Our Vision

Respectful agriculture

Our farm, Les Jardins de Valentin, is part of a human, respectful and fraternal vision of permaculture. Also called sustainable agriculture, permaculture is a process of respecting the soil, nature and biodiversity. When ecosystems are observed and imitated in their natural cycles, it is possible to grow wonderful fruits and vegetables while respecting the environment and the health of people.


Taking care of your health with food

Hippocrates, the ancient Greek physician, said "Let your food be your first medicine". We have lost the taste for food and are feeding our bodies with empty nutrients. The World Health Organization has a lot of information about nutrition. To regain full health and vitality, we must absolutely put life back on our plates. That means vegetables at every meal and fruit every day!


Treat yourself to healthy foods

No, vegetables are not boring! They can be so tasty and full of flavor. World renowned chefs Jamie Olivier and Yotam Ottolenghi have vegetarian recipe blogs. Check them out and learn how to cook vegetables and spices for the benefit of your family and your health!
